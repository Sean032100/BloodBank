Requirements by Blood Donation Type

These are some of the requirements donors must meet to be eligible to donate blood based on their donation type

Donation Frequency is Every 56 Days

You must be in Good Health & Feeling Well

You must be atleast 16 years old

You must weigh atleast 110lbs

Donation Process

The donation process from the time you arrive center until the time you leave

1 - Registration

Register an account then book an appointment. Once approved, you'll be given a schedule for blood donation

2 - Seeing

You will be examine by the physician if you are qualified for blood donation then if you are qualified, the blood extraction will be perform

3 - Donation

After donating blood, you’ll have a snack and something to drink in the refreshment area. You’ll leave after 10-15 minutes and continue your normal routine

4 - Save Life

Enjoy the feeling of accomplishment knowing you are helping to save lives. Take a selfie, or simply share your good deed with friends. It may inspire them to become blood donors
